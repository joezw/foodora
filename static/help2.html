Welcome To Foodora!

Making Ordering Easier


Why Do I need Foodora?

Foodora helps you choose what to order at a new restaurant.

How does Foodora work you ask?


What happens when you go to a new restaurant but neither you or your friends have every been there. That is where Foodora comes in. You pull up Foodora enter the restaurant you are at and we will give you food that people with similar tastes have enjoyed.

How do you do this?


When you go to a restaurant, you can rate the food you eat. To do this start the app and go to the ratings page. Once there enter in the restaurant you are at and then the food. Submit those and then enter the ratings from 1 to 5.
This allows us to build up a profile of your likes and dislikes. This will allow us to choose food you will like at new restaurants.

When you go to a new restaurant start the app and click the button new restaurant. Once there enter the restaurant you are out, allow for a few seconds while we figure out what you will like, then see the top three choices that we choose for you. Its easy and will allow for less frustration when going to a new restaurant.

What if I want to change my ratings or see my current ratings for food?

In order to do this go to the favorites page. From there you can see your top picks and pictures that you have assigned to each one. Go to the food you wish to re-rate, change the rating and click submit. This will change the current rating to the newest rating.
